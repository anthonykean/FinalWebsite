Overview

The Miami Hurricanes are coming off of a very successful season under Head Coach Mark Richt. After getting off to a 10-0 start, the Canes stumbled towards the end losing their final three games. Coming off a season where Miami was ranked as high as 2nd in the Nation, the Canes have high hopes for this season.

Spring Football!

Miami Hurricanes Spring Game is on April 14th at 6 P.M. at Hard Rock Stadium. The spring game is an open event for fans and a great opportunity for a sneak peak of this years team in action.
